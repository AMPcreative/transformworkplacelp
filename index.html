Are your interns ready
for the workplace?

Transform Workplaces is an e-learning experience designed to empower student interns to cultivate respectful, inclusive environments free from workplace harassment.

Today's workplace is complex.

And sexual harassment is a complex issue faced by many interns. Transform Workplaces  is an e-learning experience developed with the conviction that we can do more than train interns to identify instances of sexual harassment in the workplace — we can prepare them to be catalysts for change.  Our narrative based training goes beyond defining and recognizing sexual harassment.  It uses research-based best practices to give interns practical skills to help them make a positive impact in tough situations, including:

  • Interrupting unprofessional, and potentially harassing behavior. 
  • Supporting targets of sexual harassment with ally behavior and knowledge of organizational reporting processes. 
  • Recognizing resources that help interns who are involved in instances of sexual harassment. 
  • Modeling behavior through engaging narratives that guide interns in their own professional development.

Features

Social Media-Inspired Video Delivery

Student interns relate to the content, delivered via a video and messaging-styled interface.

Narrative Learning

New knowledge is embedded in a compelling story that spans the experience.

Interactive Design

Learners can check their understanding and get immediate feedback.

Online Delivery

We host the experience online, allowing your interns to easily access and complete it.

Robust Data Tracking

See who has begun and who has completed the training. View final assessment scores.

Evidence-based approaches

We conduct research and refine Transform Workplaces based on our findings.

Regular content updates

Changes in policy and new research findings are incorporated periodically.

Take at once, or in several sittings

The one-hour experience is divided into 8 modules. Learners can go through all 8 in one sitting, or start and stop as needed.
